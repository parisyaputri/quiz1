Food Recommendation

Selat Solo

Selat Solo stands as an emblematic Javanese offering closely tied to Solo's gastronomic legacy. This substantial dish traditionally incorporates beef, potatoes, carrots, and green beans bathed in a luscious, savory sauce. Frequently adorned with hard-boiled eggs and crisped shallots, Selat Solo harmoniously blends sweet, savory, and gently spicy notes.

Nasi Liwet

Nasi Liwet represents a Javanese rice tradition that embodies the straightforward and cordial essence of Solo's culture. The rice is meticulously cooked in coconut milk and infused with pandan leaves, imparting a delightful, slightly sweet fragrance. Typically served with an array of accompaniments such as fried
chicken, tempeh, and sambal (spicy chili sauce), Nasi Liwet offers a diverse ensemble of flavors.

Soto Triwindu

Soto Triwindu is a comforting Javanese broth celebrated for its robust, flavorful base. It showcases shredded chicken, rice noodles, and bean sprouts, all enveloped in a fragrant and savory broth. Complemented by lime, celery leaves, and crisped shallots, this cherished dish elevates the dining experience with its nuanced flavors.
